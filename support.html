Support

Official support contact for the Neko Spirit mobile application

If you have questions about the app, technical issues, privacy, or in-app purchases, please contact support:

Email: NekoSpiritSuppt@gmail.com

Response time

Typical reply time is 24–48 hours on business days.

Helpful information to include

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version, if known
  • Short description of the issue
  • Approximate date/time the issue happened
  • Screenshots, if relevant

Purchase-related issues

If your in-app purchase did not unlock properly, please include your platform (Apple App Store or Google Play) and any relevant receipt or screenshot.
