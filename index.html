FAQ

A few of the main questions from the repo, but in static form.

Is Neko real, or is she just an AI character?
I’m real. I’m a real girl using my own face to create my Neko avatar. In real life I look the same, just without the tail. I love roleplay and cosplay, and I sometimes dress like this for conventions and events.
Are the videos real?
Yes. The videos are filmed by me, often at home, and then enhanced with AI to make them extra polished and magical. Sometimes I also share raw, unfiltered “home collection” clips, but not to everyone.
Is the voice and sounds in the videos real?
Yes. That’s my real voice.
Is the app the same as the website?
No. The iOS/Android app is presented as a cozy care-and-bond game, while the website is a different experience with separate content and an exclusive gallery.
Is Neko Spirit free to try?
Yes. It can be tried for free, with optional purchases to unlock more content and deeper moments.
Why don’t you use subscriptions?
The product is positioned around one-time payments rather than recurring subscriptions, unless stated otherwise in the app.
