Privacy Policy — Neko Spirit

Effective date: 23.02.2026 · Last updated: 23.02.2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Oskars Putnins collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the Neko Spirit mobile application and related supporting services.

Publisher / Data Controller

Oskars Putnins
Contact email: NekoSpiritSuppt@gmail.com
Location: Latvia (EU)

Who this applies to

The app is intended for adults aged 18+. It is not directed to children, and the policy states that personal information is not knowingly collected from anyone under 18.

Information you provide

The project states that in-app text such as a name for gameplay may be entered locally. It also says that no username/password account registration is required to use the app.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage and interaction events
  • Technical identifiers

Purchases

The app offers one-time in-app purchases, not subscriptions. Purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. The policy says full payment card details are not received by the publisher.

Questions

For privacy-related questions, contact NekoSpiritSuppt@gmail.com.
