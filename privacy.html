Privacy Policy — Neko Spirit

Effective date: 23.02.2026 · Last updated: 23.02.2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Oskars Putnins (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the Neko Spirit mobile application (the “App”).

By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

1. Scope

This Policy applies to information processed through the App and related services that support the App (for example, content delivery and diagnostics). It does not apply to third-party services you access via links in the App (e.g., Instagram, TikTok), which have their own privacy policies.

2. Age Restriction (18+)

The App is intended for adults (18+). The App is not directed to children and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has used the App and provided personal data, contact us and we will take reasonable steps to delete it where applicable.

3. What the App Is (and Is Not)

The App is an entertainment-focused companion experience.

The App is not a dating service, escort service, or a platform for arranging real-world sexual services, and it does not permit content involving minors.

4. Information We Collect

We collect minimal technical data necessary to operate, improve, and secure the App, and to deliver purchased content.

4.1 Information you provide

In-app text (local gameplay input): You may enter a name or similar text for in-game use. This is primarily used inside the App experience.

Note: Depending on your device settings and backup/sync features (e.g., Apple iCloud backup / Google backup), some local app data may be backed up by your platform provider.

We do not require account registration (no username/password login) to use the App.

4.2 Information collected automatically (technical & usage data)

We may collect:

  • Device and app information: device model, operating system version, App version, language/locale, approximate region derived from device settings or network signals, and performance metrics.
  • App usage and interaction events: screens viewed, buttons tapped, session duration, feature usage, and viewing behavior (e.g., which content areas are opened).
  • Identifiers: app instance identifiers and similar technical identifiers used by analytics and crash diagnostics.
  • Crash and diagnostics data: crash logs, stack traces, and limited device state information at the time of a crash.

4.3 Purchases and purchased content delivery

The App offers one-time in-app purchases (no subscriptions). Purchases are processed by Apple App Store / Google Play. We may process:

  • Purchase status / entitlement signals necessary to unlock purchased content and support “Restore Purchases.”
  • Content delivery logs when you download purchased video packs (e.g., request timestamps, basic network information).

We do not receive your full payment card details.

5. Firebase and Our Use of SDKs

We use Google Firebase services to support the App, including:

  • Firebase Storage – to store and deliver downloadable video packs.
  • Firebase Analytics – to understand how the App is used (events and engagement).
  • Firebase Crashlytics – to identify and fix crashes and stability issues.
  • Firebase Cloud Functions – to support server-side logic such as validating purchase status and controlling access to purchased downloads (“gated downloads”), and to help prevent abuse.

Firebase may process certain technical identifiers, device/app information, and usage data to provide these services.

6. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • operate the App and provide core functionality;
  • deliver purchased video packs and support “Restore Purchases”;
  • measure engagement and improve features and content (analytics);
  • detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, abuse, and security incidents;
  • troubleshoot errors and improve stability (crash reporting);
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms and policies.

7. Legal Bases for Processing (EEA/UK – GDPR)

If you are in the EEA/UK, we process personal data under these legal bases:

Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) – to provide the App features you request (e.g., delivering purchased content, restoring purchases).

Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) – to improve the App, perform analytics, maintain security, prevent abuse, and diagnose crashes.

Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) – where required (e.g., compliance requests).

Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) – only if we implement features that legally require it (e.g., certain ad tracking scenarios in the future).

Analytics opt-out: At this time, we do not offer an in-app opt-out from analytics. We aim to limit analytics to what is reasonably necessary for product improvement and stability.

8. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information:

  • With service providers that process data on our behalf (primarily Google Firebase).
  • With platform providers (Apple/Google) for purchase processing and entitlement restoration.
  • For legal and safety reasons if required by law or necessary to protect users, our rights, or to prevent abuse.
  • Business transfers (if the App or business is reorganized/sold), with appropriate safeguards.

We do not knowingly sell personal data in the traditional sense.

9. International Data Transfers

The App is offered internationally, including the EEA/UK and other regions. Our providers (including Firebase) may process information in countries outside your country of residence.

If you are in the EEA/UK and your data is transferred outside the EEA/UK, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and other measures as required.

10. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as needed for the purposes described in this Policy, then delete or anonymize it where feasible.

Typical retention periods (may vary by configuration and provider controls):

  • Firebase Analytics event data: generally up to 14 months (or as configured).
  • Crash diagnostics (Crashlytics): generally up to 90 days (or as configured) to troubleshoot stability.
  • Content delivery and security logs (if any): typically 30–90 days to prevent abuse and ensure service reliability.
  • Purchase/entitlement verification signals: retained only as needed to validate access and prevent fraud, and may be stored in aggregated/limited form.
  • Local app data can typically be removed by uninstalling the App, but platform backups (iCloud/Google backups) may retain copies depending on your device settings.

11. Your Choices and Rights

11.1 Device and platform controls

You can control certain privacy settings via your device, including:

  • disabling network access (may limit downloads and restore purchases),
  • managing notification permissions,
  • managing backup/sync settings (iCloud / Google backup),
  • resetting advertising identifiers (where relevant).

11.2 EEA/UK rights (GDPR)

You may have the right to:

  • access your personal data,
  • correct inaccurate data,
  • request deletion,
  • restrict or object to processing,
  • data portability (where applicable),
  • lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority.

Because we do not use account registration, some requests may require additional verification and may be limited by our ability to identify your data.

11.3 Right to lodge a complaint

If you are in the EEA/UK, you may lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. You may also contact us first at NekoSpiritSuppt@gmail.com.

12. Identity Verification

To protect users, we may need to verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests. Verification may include confirming access to the device, providing app details, or other reasonable steps. We only use verification information for this purpose.

13. Security

14. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Mobile apps do not use browser cookies in the same way websites do, but we and our providers may use SDK identifiers and similar technologies (e.g., app instance identifiers, device storage, and diagnostic identifiers) to:

  • provide analytics,
  • detect crashes,
  • protect against abuse,
